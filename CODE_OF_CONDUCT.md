# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make `go-prism` a practical, respectful, and safe project for Go maintainers and contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Focus criticism on code, design, evidence, and tradeoffs.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are working with limited time.
- Keep security-sensitive details out of public threads when disclosure would increase risk.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately submitting secrets, malicious code, or unsafe test targets.
- Using the project to scan or test repositories without authorization.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation.
